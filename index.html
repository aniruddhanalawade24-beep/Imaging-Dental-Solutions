OUR SERVICES

3D CBCT

3D CBCT offers detailed 3D imaging of teeth, bones, and tissues, ensuring accurate diagnosis. It's key for dental implants, Root canal treatments, Third molar surgeries, Cyst and tumour diagnosis etc.

2D OPG

2D OPG is a panoramic dental X-ray providing a 2D view of teeth, jaws, and structures, aiding in diagnosis and treatment planning.

2D LAT CEPH

2D Lateral Cephalogram is an X-ray showing a side view of the skull, used in orthodontics to assess jaw alignment, growth, and treatment planning.

2D TMJ

2D TMJ imaging provides detailed 2D views of the temporomandibular joint, aiding in diagnosing disorders, alignment, and treatment planning.
